Background

Welcome! This is a collaboration cafe with a theme of "もぐらのおうち(Mole House)", where you can be healed by the warm and cute Sumikko! “Sumikko Gurashi(“life in the corner” in English)” are the characters living a quiet and solitary life in the corner. Here is a video about them. In this video, a new character named Mole is introduced.

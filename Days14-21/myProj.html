Fall20 ITS Project: StonyScape

For many college freshman, the idea of moving into a dorm, getting involved, and making new friends are all challenges on their own, but to do so all at once may seem very daunting.
All of this along with a pandemic can be very stressful for college freshman in an already stressful time of their lives. Our goal is to create a service that will make this process much smoother for these students. We hope to provide another option for students to expand their horizons and make it easier for them to adjust to the different challenges they face when starting their college life.

* We made a website of StonyScape that introduces its function, but unfortunately is not available to people that doesn't have a Stony Brook Email.

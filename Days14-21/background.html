Background

Hello, this is Choi Ying Yau!
I'm born in Hong Kong on 12/26/2002. My family and I immigrated to the United States in 2015. Though have gone through some hardships of getting used to new homeplace and language, I have learned a lot from my schools and obtained many unforgettable memories with my friends since 8th grade.
